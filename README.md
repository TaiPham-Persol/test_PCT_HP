# PERSOL CAREER TECH STUDIO VIETNAM

## Overview

This repository hosts the official website for **PERSOL CAREER TECH STUDIO VIETNAM**, a technology development center established in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, by PERSOL CAREER Co., Ltd.

PERSOL CAREER is a core company of the PERSOL Group—one of Japan's largest HR service companies, comprising 149 companies worldwide with over 70,000 employees.

## About Us

PERSOL CAREER TECH STUDIO VIETNAM is the studio to create new services related to "WORK". We are responsible for developing various service systems provided by PERSOL CAREER, including:

- **doda** - A recruitment platform with millions of users
- **PERSOL MIRAIZ** - New HR services
- **HR Spanner** - HR management solutions

## Our Mission

We focus on:

1. **Development and Operation of Major Japanese Services**
   - Developing and maintaining recruitment and career-related products
   - Supporting the development of new HR services

2. **Building and Operating Data Infrastructure**
   - Managing and maintaining databases and core systems for HR-related services

3. **Strengthening Technical Capabilities**
   - Recruiting and cultivating skilled IT talent in Vietnam
   - Closely collaborating with teams in Japan
