ABOUT US

We are the studio to create new services related to "WORK".

Why we do

Realizing a society in which more and more people can take ownership of their unique "working life".

Our mission is "GIVE PEOPLE THE POWER TO OWN THEIR WORK-LIFE". Our goal is to help each individual to correctly know, choose, and act on their own opportunities and potential. By doing so, we will realize a society in which more and more people can take ownership of their unique "working life".
How we do

Development by adopting cutting-edge methods and utilizing all kinds of technology.

We will support development by adopting cutting-edge development methods, developing borderless with Japanese development teams, and utilizing all kinds of technology; consequently, we hope to deliver good services to Japan and the rest of the world, improve the value of customer experience through technology, and support careers for each individual.
What we do

Creating new services related to "WORK".

Our studio is primarily responsible for the development of new personnel-related services of PERSOL CAREER CO., LTD., and the maintenance of the database systems that form the core of those services. The parent company, PERSOL CAREER CO., LTD., is one of the core companies of PERSOL HOLDINGS CO., LTD., which is the No. 2 recruitment agency in Japan.

Message

We aim to establish the world's top development team in Vietnam to realize Persol Career's mission of "GIVE PEOPLE THE POWER TO OWN THEIR WORK-LIFE". HERE in Vietnam, through technology, we aspire to create a society where each individual can live a more vibrant and fulfilling life.
We believe in people's potential more than anyone else and fully support each person's life from the perspective of their work. This commitment extends equally to our colleagues with whom we work together.

Masatoshi Yamamoto / General Director
