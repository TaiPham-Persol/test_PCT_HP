PERSOL CAREER TECH STUDIO VIETNAM

Our Business

PERSOL CAREER TECH STUDIO VIETNAM is a technology development center established in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, by PERSOL CAREER Co., Ltd., a core company of the PERSOL Groupone of Japan's largest HR service companies, comprising 149 companies worldwide with over 70,000 employees.

The studio is responsible for developing various service systems provided by PERSOL CAREER, including "doda," a recruitment platform with approximately 9.5 million users.

Our Strength

01

Development and Operation of Major Japanese Services

  • Developing and maintaining recruitment and career-related products, including the "doda" service provided by PERSOL CAREER.

  • Supporting the development of new HR services such as "PERSOL MIRAIZ" and "HR Spanner".

02

Beyond Development: Building and Operating Data Infrastructure

  • Managing and maintaining databases and core systems for HR-related services.

03

Strengthening Technical Capabilities

  • Recruiting and cultivating skilled IT talent in Vietnam to internalize development, closely collaborating with teams in Japan.

  • Establishing a collaborative environment for sharing advanced technical skills and mutual learning between Vietnam and Japan.

04

Supporting In-House Development

  • Establishing an internal development framework to build and retain technical expertise, reducing dependency on external partners.

  • Expanding the Vietnam-based development team to approximately 70 members,aiming scale up 150 in the future.

Background and Advantages of Vietnam Base

PERSOL CAREER TECH STUDIO VIETNAM was established to build internal expertise and experience in software development, driven by the shortage of IT talent in Japan and the need to shift away from relying heavily on external partners. Vietnam, which nationally promotes IT talent development, is expected to be an excellent source of skilled professionals. Additionally, the minimal two-hour time difference with Japan enables swift and efficient communication. PERSOL CAREER TECH STUDIO VIETNAM is more than just a software development center; it functions as an integral part of PERSOL CAREERs internal development team. Japanese and Vietnamese engineers collaborate closely to drive service development. Moving forward, the studio is expected to contribute further to the realization of PERSOL CAREER's mission by expanding the organization and strengthening its development capabilities.

Service & Product

doda

This is a leading mid-career job change platform in Japan, boasting one of the highest numbers of users in the industry. As of the end of January 2025, it has 9.43 million registered members and is widely used by people primarily in their 20s to 30s. It serves as PERSOL CAREERs core service, addressing the needs of both individuals seeking new career opportunities and companies advancing their hiring activities.
