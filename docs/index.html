PERSOL CAREER TECH STUDIO VIETNAM

Our Business

PERSOL CAREER TECH STUDIO VIETNAM is a technology development center established in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, by PERSOL CAREER Co., Ltd., a core company of the PERSOL Group—one of Japan's largest HR service companies, comprising 149 companies worldwide with over 70,000 employees.

The studio is responsible for developing various service systems provided by PERSOL CAREER, including "doda," a recruitment platform with approximately 9.5 million users.
